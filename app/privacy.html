Privacy Policy

At Asiacrypto Agency, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights related to that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website, submit a form, or engage with our services:

  • • Contact Information — name, email address, Telegram handle, etc.
  • • Project Details — campaign goals, assets, materials you provide.
  • • Technical Data — IP address, browser type, device info, and cookies.
  • • Communication Data — messages sent via forms, email, or direct contact

We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes.
  • • Deliver marketing services and run campaigns.
  • • Communicate with you about updates or support.
  • • Improve our website and service offerings.
  • • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • • Influencers or campaign partners, only as needed to deliver services.
  • • Service providers, such as hosting or analytics tools.
  • • Legal authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • • All third parties are required to handle your data securely and only for intended purposes.
4. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined above or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

5. Cookies and Trackin

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website functionality and analyze usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • • Correct or update your data
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Object to certain uses of your data
  • • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites or platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
